Our Services

IELTS Training

Taking the English competence examination can be a difficult undertaking. That is why we offer top-notch training to ensure you pass the exam on your first try!

NCK Verification

We will follow up with your documents to ensure that you are verified and certified by the Nursing Council of Kenya within hours!

New NMC CBT Training

We understand that the Computer-Based Test by the Nursing and Midwifery Commission of the UK is daunting. This is why we will train you to pass it on your first try!

International CV Alignment

A Curriculum Vitae can increase or decrease your chances of getting hired by your next employer. Leave it to us to proofread, edit and align it in accordance with what UK employers prefer.

OET Training

We prepare our candidates to take the Occupational English Test and pass it on their first try!

TOEFL Training

Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) is a test to measure the English language ability of non-native speakers.

Job Interviews Assitance

We will train you on everything you need to know before sitting for an interview with your prospective employer.

Passport Application

Applying for a passport in Kenya is not for the faint-hearted. We will make this process seem like a breeze for you.

International University Placement

Ever wanted to advance your career by studying in the UK, the US, or other countries? We will make this a reality for you.

NMC Account Creation

We understand that the NMC website may not be user-friendly. That is why we will open an account for you as you focus on more meaningful goals.

IELTS/OET/NMC payments

Having trouble with IELTS/OET/NMC payments? We will assist you so that you can focus on passing the tests.

IELTS Units Assistance

You may have trouble with a specific units between writing, speaking, listening and reading. Just pay for that unit and let's train you
